SPADS - Spatial Analysis and Decision System

SPADS APP-GORODA 1 is a pedagogical and technical consultation platform that combines geo-spatial analysis, computing with open-source tools, stakeholder participation and a deep and thorough understanding of the field derived from actual cases encountered in our professional work.

As the world steadily urbanises, the myriad challenges facing our cities and regions have grown in size and complexity. The measures adopted by humanity to tackle these challenges - be they related to economic development, climate action, poverty alleviation or governance - are also becoming increasingly complex, inter-related and vast in their size and scope. Spatial and non-spatial elements of change interact in uncertain and unpredictable ways both in the present instant and over time.

Successfully addressing the challenges facing our cities and regions and managing the human responses to such problems, requires an approach to analysis and decision-making that is fluid, dynamic and creative.
